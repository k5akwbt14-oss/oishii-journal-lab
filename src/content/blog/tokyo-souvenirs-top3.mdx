## Buying tips before you check out

<Callout title="🧳 Don't waste a good souvenir">
- **Buy last, not first.** All three taste best within a few days — grab them on the way to the airport.
- **Airport prices match the city.** Haneda and Narita carry the same boxes at the same prices. No need to panic-buy early.
- **In summer, ask for an ice pack with the creamy ones.** Most shops add one for free.
- **Check the best-by date,** especially if the box needs to last more than 2–3 days.
- **Prices above are Tokyo store prices.** The "check price" buttons lead to import listings in the US, which typically run 2–4× the local price — that's the shipping, not a scam.
</Callout>
