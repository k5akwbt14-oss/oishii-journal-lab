## 🎁 Bokksu — best for authentic discovery

Bokksu is the "premium discovery" box. You get 20–22 snacks from small, regional Japanese makers — the kind you genuinely can't find outside Japan — plus a tea and a genuinely useful Culture Guide that explains every item.

**Best for:** first-timers and gift-givers who want authentic, story-rich snacks over mainstream candy.

## 🍵 Sakuraco — best for tradition & tea

Sakuraco leans traditional. Think **wagashi** (classic Japanese confections) from local artisans, a tea pairing, and — the standout — a **handmade homeware item** each month like ceramics, chopsticks, or a furoshiki cloth. It's less about novelty and more about a calm, cultural ritual.

**Best for:** people who love tea time, traditional sweets, and a keepsake they'll actually use.

## 🎉 TokyoTreat — best for fun & pop culture

TokyoTreat is the party box. It's packed with **Japan-exclusive, limited-edition, and pop-culture snacks** — often bigger, louder flavors — plus a **Japanese drink or soda** and sometimes a DIY candy kit. Themed around things like Akihabara and seasonal releases.

**Best for:** families, kids, and anyone who wants the *fun*, viral, "what even is this" snack experience.

## The verdict

There's no single winner — there's a winner *for you*. For most first-timers and gift-givers, **Bokksu** is the safest, most rewarding pick thanks to its authentic curation and Culture Guide. Choose **Sakuraco** for tradition and tea, or **TokyoTreat** for pure pop-culture fun.
